A Little About Myself

Hi, my name is Kevin Tang. I am a recent graduate from the University of Illinois at Chicago, having obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a Software Engineering concentration.

Even post-graduation, the education and learning doesn't end, and that's something for me to look forward to. As someone always seeking new knowledge and self-motivated to constantly better myself, I make the opportunities happen, and there are plenty in the world that I see.

On my journey in exploring the several facets of the tech industry, I have started off my career working as a QA Engineer, picking up a wide variety of tools, methodologies, and experiences.

Passion

Simply put, video games have been undoubtedly one of the biggest factors in my personal development. From sitting on my living room floor playing party games with my family, to competing in multiple tournaments and leading large communities, my passion for gaming has made me who I am today. It was the absolute deciding factor for me to pursue a Computer Science degree, and what connected me to some of my closest friends. Every aspect of video games, including the gameplay, the art, the competition, the people, and even the losses are something that I have grown to constantly appreciate.

Nowadays, you'll probably catch me putting free time into World of Warcraft, Overwatch, Counter-Strike, Hearthstone, or maybe battling it out in Street Fighter or Marvel vs Capcom at a local gaming lounge. Some personal achievements of mine include placing first in more than 20 local and regional tournaments for Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, and being a founding member and leader of a server rank 1 raiding guild in WoW.
